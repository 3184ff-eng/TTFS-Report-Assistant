# Fire Report Vetting Checklist

Placeholder for the Fire Report Vetting Checklist.

## Checklist Areas

- Required fields
- Cause classification
- Officer's Observations
- Additional Information
- Chronology
- Completeness
- Clarity
- Missing or unconfirmed facts

## Mandatory Administrative Data

Check that the report contains the report number, station, watch, date call received, time call received, how call was received, address given, actual address, time appliance left station, date of fire, date of report, reporting officer, and rank.

## Incident Description

Check that the property description is sufficient for Fire Prevention review. A strong description includes property use, construction type or class where known, storeys, approximate dimensions where known, room or area involved, owner/occupier, and exposures.

## Operational Detail

Check that appliances attending, officers attending, FS/SO and FS/O attending, number of men attending, professional count, auxiliary count, hydrant distance, water supply, LPM available, LPM required, and extinguishment method are complete or flagged.

## Observations Versus Additional Information

Officer's Observations must not include statements from witnesses, owners, police, ambulance, callers, or other persons. Received information and pre-arrival actions must be moved to Additional Information.

## Damage, Values, Insurance, And Casualties

Check that damage description is clear and that value/damage/insurance figures are entered in separate official fields:

- Value of Building
- Value of Stock
- Damage to Building
- Damage to Stock
- Building and Stock Insured as follows

Casualties must be entered in the casualty table by name, brief description of injuries, and treated by.

## Cause Analysis

Check that cause classification is one of Natural, Accidental, Incendiary, or Undetermined. Unsupported cause wording must be flagged. Do not infer cause from weak or incomplete notes.
